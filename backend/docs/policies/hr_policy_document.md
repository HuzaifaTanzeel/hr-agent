## 1. Leave Policy

### 1.1 Annual Leave
All full-time employees are entitled to **20 days of annual leave** per calendar year. Annual leave accrues on a monthly basis at a rate of 1.67 days per month. Employees can carry forward up to 5 days of unused annual leave to the next calendar year, subject to manager approval.

**Requesting Annual Leave:**
- Annual leave requests must be submitted at least 2 weeks in advance for periods longer than 5 consecutive days
- For shorter periods, a minimum of 3 business days' notice is required
- All leave requests require manager approval before being processed
- During peak business periods, leave may be restricted to ensure adequate staffing

### 1.2 Sick Leave
Employees are entitled to **10 days of paid sick leave** per calendar year. Sick leave can be used for personal illness, medical appointments, or caring for an immediate family member who is ill.

**Sick Leave Guidelines:**
- For absences of 3 or more consecutive days, a medical certificate is required
- Sick leave should be reported to your manager as soon as possible, preferably before your scheduled start time
- Unused sick leave does not carry forward to the next year
- In cases of extended illness, employees may be eligible for additional unpaid leave or disability benefits

### 1.3 Casual Leave
Employees are entitled to **5 days of casual leave** per calendar year for personal matters that cannot be scheduled in advance. Casual leave can be used for emergencies, personal appointments, or other urgent personal matters.

**Casual Leave Guidelines:**
- Casual leave requests should be submitted with as much advance notice as possible
- Manager approval is required for all casual leave requests
- Casual leave cannot be carried forward to the next year
- Casual leave should not be used for planned vacations (use annual leave instead)

### 1.4 Unpaid Leave
Unpaid leave may be granted for extended personal circumstances, educational purposes, or other approved reasons. Unpaid leave requires written application and senior management approval.

**Unpaid Leave Guidelines:**
- Minimum request period: 1 week
- Maximum continuous unpaid leave: 3 months (subject to business needs)
- Health insurance and other benefits may be affected during unpaid leave
- Employees must discuss unpaid leave with HR and their manager before submitting a formal request

### 1.5 Leave Balance and Carry Forward
- Leave balances are calculated on a calendar year basis (January 1 to December 31)
- Employees can check their leave balance at any time through the HR portal
- Leave balances reset on January 1st of each year
- Only annual leave can be carried forward (up to 5 days with approval)

## 2. Work Arrangements

### 2.1 Work-from-Home Policy
The company supports flexible work arrangements, including remote work options. Employees may work from home up to **3 days per week** with manager approval.

**Work-from-Home Guidelines:**
- Remote work requests must be approved by your direct manager
- Employees must have a suitable home office setup with reliable internet connection
- Core business hours (10:00 AM - 3:00 PM) must be maintained for team collaboration
- Regular in-person team meetings may be required
- Performance expectations remain the same regardless of work location

### 2.2 Hybrid Work Model
The company operates a hybrid work model combining office and remote work. Employees are expected to be in the office at least **2 days per week** for team collaboration and meetings.

**Hybrid Work Requirements:**
- Office days should be coordinated with your team to maximize collaboration
- Meeting rooms and collaboration spaces are available for in-person work
- IT support is available both in-office and remotely
- Flexible hours are available, with core hours of 10:00 AM - 3:00 PM

### 2.3 Flexible Working Hours
Employees may adjust their working hours within the flexible hours framework (7:00 AM - 7:00 PM) to accommodate personal needs, subject to business requirements and manager approval.

**Flexible Hours Guidelines:**
- Standard workday is 8 hours (excluding lunch break)
- Core collaboration hours (10:00 AM - 3:00 PM) must be maintained
- Flexible hours must be approved by your manager
- Employees must ensure adequate coverage for their role during business hours

## 3. Code of Conduct

### 3.1 Professional Behavior
All employees are expected to maintain the highest standards of professional conduct, including respect for colleagues, clients, and company property.

### 3.2 Attendance and Punctuality
Regular attendance and punctuality are essential. Employees are expected to arrive on time and notify their manager in case of lateness or absence.

### 3.3 Confidentiality
Employees must maintain confidentiality of company information, client data, and proprietary information both during and after employment.

## 4. Benefits and Compensation

### 4.1 Health Insurance
The company provides comprehensive health insurance coverage for all full-time employees and their dependents. Coverage includes medical, dental, and vision benefits.

### 4.2 Retirement Benefits
Employees are eligible for the company's 401(k) retirement plan with employer matching up to 5% of salary after completing 6 months of service.

### 4.3 Professional Development
The company encourages professional growth and provides:
- Annual training budget of $1,000 per employee
- Conference attendance opportunities
- Internal training programs
- Mentorship programs
